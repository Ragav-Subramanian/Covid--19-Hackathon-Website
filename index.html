ABOUT CONTENT

Covid 19 Solutions, provides a wide range of applications that helps the user to utilize the services delivered by the Government of Telangana . The Corona Pandemic has created an atmosphere where going out of your home isn't a safe decision. Therefore, in order to avoid the movement of people we provide you with the necessary service from the comfort of your home.

The Government of Telangana has been working hard in dealing with the pandemic by providing necessary arrangements to their people. Covid 19 Solutions aims in supporting the vision of Telangana in helping people through our applications. Chief Minister K Chandrashekhar Rao, promises every Individual of Telangana that the state will be Corona Free soon.

The main Products of Covid 19 Solutions deals with

1.Essential Pass Generation: Anyone who has an urgent need of pass need not wait but register in our app with the required documents wherein the details will be verified and a pass will be generated by the government which will result in a hassle free travel during odd times. "This Pass is for an individual."

2.Vehicle Pass Generation: Any vehicle that carries essential elements or during emergency can apply for a pass for a limited number of days and has to renew it . This can help the police of Telangana in verifying vehicles that are permitted and not put fine on them.

3.Doctor's Online appointment: In this application,we allow both patients and doctors to register and according to the patient's needs a doctor is assigned. The doctor already has the details submitted by the Patient making it time efficient while visiting.

4.Labour Shelter Mapping: This helps in finding the nearest shelter that is availabe . It helps migrants and labours to find a temporary stay until Government decides on other solutions in evacuating them.Telangana assures the safety of the people who are in their state.
